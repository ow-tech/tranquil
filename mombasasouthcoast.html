Shimba Hills & Shedrick Falls

Shimba Hills National Reserve & Sheldrick Falls
Day Experience Escape the city and sandy beaches to reconnect with nature on our Shimba Hills National Reserve & Sheldrick Falls Day Trip, a carefully curated experience by Tranquil Treks & Motion. Located just a short drive from Mombasa, Shimba Hills offers a peaceful retreat into one of Kenya’s most scenic coastal forest reserves. Your journey begins with an early, comfortable departure, ensuring a full day of exploration and relaxation. This fully inclusive experience offers seamless hotel transfers, expert safari guidance, park access fees, and a smooth game-viewing drive through one of Kenya’s most picturesque reserves. Take in sweeping coastal vistas, savor a well-prepared lunch, and enjoy memorable moments in this exceptional wildlife destination near Diani Beach. Perfect for couples, families, and independent travelers, this journey delivers an immersive and genuine Kenyan safari experience.

These activities include water sports and beachside relaxation. Whether it's kite surfing, snorkeling, or simply basking in the sun, Diani Beach has it all.

Important info that you should know before you visit this place.

Wildlife: Rich with elephants, colobus monkeys, and birds.
Waterfalls: Kendric Falls offers stunning views and cool water.
ATrails: Scenic hiking paths through lush forests.
Climate: Cooler temperatures, refreshing escape.
Culture: Sacred sites with local historical significance.
Accessibility : Easily reachable via guided tours.
